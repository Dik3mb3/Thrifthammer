About ThriftHammer

{# ── Mission ── #}

Our Mission

Stop overpaying for plastic.

ThriftHammer is dedicated to helping the Warhammer community find the best deals on the hobby games and models they love. Every penny saved on a kit is a penny that can go toward the next one — whether that's more infantry, a centrepiece model, or that paint set you've been eyeing for months.

We believe the hobby should be as accessible as possible. The models are already expensive — you shouldn't have to pay more than you need to. Our goal is simple: give every hobbyist, whether they're building their first Combat Patrol or their fifth army, the information they need to stretch their budget as far as it'll go.

We track prices across US retailers so you always know exactly where to buy, when to buy, and how much you'll save — without having to open twenty browser tabs yourself.

{# ── What we do ── #}

What ThriftHammer Does

Price Tracking

We monitor prices from Miniature Market, Noble Knight Games, eBay, Amazon, and Games Workshop itself. Every product page shows you a live side-by-side comparison so the best deal is always obvious at a glance.

Watchlist Alerts

Add any kit to your personal watchlist. When the price drops, you'll know. No more manually checking retailer sites every week hoping something has gone on sale — we do the watching so you can focus on painting.

Army Cost Calculator

Planning a new army? Use our Army Calculator to build your roster unit by unit and see exactly what it'll cost across all tracked retailers before you spend a single dollar. Know the real price of your hobby before you commit.

Collection Tracker

Log what you own, what you've spent, and what you've saved versus GW retail. Your collection dashboard gives you a running total of everything in your hobby room — assembled, painted, or still waiting in the pile of shame.

{# ── Who built this ── #}

Who Built This

Hi — I'm the person behind ThriftHammer. I'm a relatively new player to the hobby, been in the community for a few years now. I predominantly play Orks (Waagh!), but I've expanded my collection into Grey Knights, T'au, and Necrons along the way.

I'm currently married and just recently had a beautiful baby boy who I hope to share this hobby with as he gets older — maybe he'll be rolling dice across from me one day.

ThriftHammer was born out of my own early struggles navigating what is an amazing but very expensive hobby. I spent too many hours jumping between browser tabs trying to figure out where to get the best price on a kit. I figured if I needed this tool, others probably did too — so I built it.

If this site is successful, I hope to reinvest any proceeds from affiliates into new tools and features for all our users. I hope you join me on this journey. Please reach out if you spot any pricing errors, bugs, or just want to say hello — I genuinely want to hear from you.

This is an independent project with no affiliation to Games Workshop, Citadel Miniatures, or any of the retailers we track.
