Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Who We Are

ThriftHammer ("we", "us", "our") is a Warhammer price comparison website. We help hobbyists find the best prices on Games Workshop products across US retailers. ThriftHammer is not affiliated with or endorsed by Games Workshop Ltd.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account registration: username and email address
  • Watchlist items and army lists you create
  • Collection entries you add

Information collected automatically

  • Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Session cookies required for login functionality
  • Django CSRF tokens for form security

We do not collect payment information, run advertising tracking pixels, or sell your data to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain your account
  • To save your watchlist, army lists, and collection
  • To send price drop alerts if you opt in
  • To improve the site's performance and content

4. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies only:

  • sessionid — keeps you logged in during your visit
  • csrftoken — protects forms against cross-site request forgery

We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking scripts. No cookie consent banner is required as we only use technically necessary cookies.

5. Affiliate Relationships & Advertising Disclosures

ThriftHammer participates in affiliate advertising programs. This means that when you click certain links on this site and make a purchase, we may earn a commission — at no additional cost to you. Affiliate commissions help keep ThriftHammer free to use.

All prices shown are for informational purposes only. Always verify the current price on the retailer's website before purchasing.

Amazon Associates

As an Amazon Associate, ThriftHammer earns from qualifying purchases. Links to Amazon products may be affiliate links.

eBay Partner Network

ThriftHammer is a member of the eBay Partner Network (EPN). When you click a link to eBay from this site and make a qualifying purchase or bid, we may receive a commission. eBay and the eBay logo are trademarks of eBay Inc. ThriftHammer is not affiliated with or endorsed by eBay Inc. Links to eBay listings may be affiliate links marked with #ad or #CommissionsEarned.

Miniature Market

ThriftHammer participates in the Miniature Market affiliate program, managed through Impact. When you click a link to Miniature Market from this site and make a purchase, we may receive a commission. Miniature Market is an independent retailer not affiliated with Games Workshop.

Other Retailers

We may also include affiliate or referral links to other Warhammer retailers including Noble Knight Games, Tower of Games, and others. Any such links will be clearly identified. Commissions received do not influence how products are ranked or displayed on this site — we always show the cheapest price first regardless of retailer.

6. FTC Disclosure Compliance

In accordance with the US Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255), ThriftHammer clearly discloses all material connections with advertisers and affiliate partners. Affiliate links on this site are identified as paid links. Disclosures appear on product pages adjacent to affiliate links, and site-wide in this policy and the site footer.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us at the address below. Server logs are retained for up to 30 days for security purposes.

8. Third-Party Services

ThriftHammer is hosted on Railway.app. Their privacy policy applies to infrastructure-level data handling. We do not embed third-party advertising, analytics scripts, or social media tracking widgets.

9. Children's Privacy

ThriftHammer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us via the email below. We will respond within 30 days.
